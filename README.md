# Financial Calculator 

A Calculator to know returns of your investments.

## Getting Started

Here’s what you can do with this app:

1.       **Lumpsum Investment**: Calculate the returns on a one-time investment over a period.
2.       **Fixed Deposit**: Find out how much your fixed deposits will grow.
3.       **Recurring Deposit**: See the growth of regular deposits made over time.
4.       **SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)**: Calculate the returns on regular, smaller mutual fund investments.
5.       **Step Up Investment (Step-Up SIP)**: Calculate returns with increasing mutual fund SIP investments over time.
6.       **EMI (Equated Monthly Instalment)**: Plan and calculate your loan repayments.
7.       **Goal Planning** : Plan your financial goals with SIP or a one-time investment.
8.       **FIRE Number Calculator**: Find out how much you need to achieve Financial Independence, Retire Early.
9.       **Step Up Investment**: Calculate returns with increasing investments over time.
10.      **Time Duration Regular and Lump Sum**: Determine returns for different investment periods, both for regular and one-time investments.
11.      **CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate)**: Measure the annual growth rate of your investments over time.

## Usage
- Simply click on the calculator which you want to use. 
- Enter the details and click **Calculate**. 
- All the calculators have a **RESET** button which clears the input provided.
